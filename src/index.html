Data Visualization: Programming Assignment 1

Estimated Annual Global Mean Temperatures from 1880 through 2017

Interactive chart shows mean temperatures from 1880 through 1937 below zero degrees Celsius. Temperatures from 1938 through 1976 are mostly below zero degrees Celsius with a few exceptions. From 1977 through 2017 all temperatures are above zero degrees Celsius.

Explore the Data

To zoom into a section of the data, either scroll over the large chart or adjust the gray area on the overlay section below the table.

Explanation

The chart depicts the estimated global mean temperatures from 1880 through 2017 in Celsius. Overall the global means have increased from 1880. Starting in 1977 the temperatures have all been over zero degrees Celsius. This trend could be caused by industrialization and the rise of population.

Data Source

National Aeronautics and Space Administration Goddard Institute for Space Studies. (2017). Global-mean Monthly, Seasonal, and Annual Means [Data File]. Retrieved August 23, 2017, from https://data.giss.nasa.gov/gistemp/
