Data Visualization: Programming Assignment 1

Estimated Annual Global Mean Temperatures from 1880 through 2017

Explanation

The chart depicts the estimated global mean temperatures from 1880 through 2017 in celsius.

Data Source

National Aeronautics and Space Administration Goddard Institute for Space Studies. (2017). Global-mean Monthly, Seasonal, and Annual Means [Data File]. Retrieved August 23, 2017, from https://data.giss.nasa.gov/gistemp/
